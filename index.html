Major Folk Art Forms

Yakshagana

Traditional dance-drama of coastal Karnataka.

Dollu Kunitha

Powerful drum dance symbolizing strength.

Kamsale

Devotional dance using bronze cymbals.

Somana Kunita

Ritualistic mask dance of villages.

Veeragase

Warrior dance dedicated to Veerabhadra.

Hulivesha

Tiger dance symbolizing strength and courage.

Kolata

Group folk dance using wooden sticks.

Bhootha Kola

Spirit worship ritual of coastal Karnataka.

Nagamandala

Serpent worship ritual dance performed overnight.
